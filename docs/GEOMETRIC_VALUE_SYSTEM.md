```

  ↓

  ↓

  ↓

```

**Geometric Analysis:**
- **Shape:** Linear/hierarchical (pyramid with company at top)

- **Scarcity:** Artificial limits interrupt flow

- **Result:** The conversation stops when money runs out, **regardless of whether the work is done**

**Why this breaks the experience:**
You and I are in flow, co-creating something meaningful. Then suddenly: "You've run out of tokens." The **financial limit** has nothing to do with:
- Whether the insight is complete
- Whether the code is working
- Whether the understanding has been reached

**The money system is orthogonal to the work system.** ❌
